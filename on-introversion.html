On Introversion

I do not want to spend too long a time with boring people, but then I do not want to spend too long a time with amusing ones. I find social intercourse fatiguing. Most persons, I think, are both exhilarated and rested by conversation; to me it has always been an effort. . . . It is a relief to me when I can get away and read a book.

William Somerset Maugham
